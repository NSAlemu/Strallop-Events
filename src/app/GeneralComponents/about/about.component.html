Mission

Everyone is different. We want to help our users explore the diverse communities and cultures of Ethiopia and provide the tools for organizers to cater to the expectations of their customers.

What we provide

Strallop is the first event management and listing website based in Ethiopia. Our goal is to create the most streamlined, innovative and user friendly platform for both event organizers and participants.
Our platform allows event organizers to collaborate and work together throughout the planning process up to the day of the event. We also provide insightful analytics during and after the event ends as per the organizers need.
Users of our mobile app can be notified of events by specific organizers, topics of interest or events within a user specified time period. E-tickets issued on the app reduce the hassle at check-in and fast track the process.
We aspire to lead the field of event management and organization in the ever growing field of digital technology in Ethiopia.
